TITLE: Heart-Safe Blend

CATEGORY: Family Heart Care Blend

COMPOSITION:
High-Oleic Sunflower Oil	45%	High MUFA, LDL reduction
Extra Virgin Olive Oil	25%	Polyphenols + antioxidant power
Rice Bran Oil	15%	Gamma-oryzanol (cholesterol lowering)
Walnut Oil	5%	Omega-3 support
Sesame Oil	10%	Lignans + oxidative protection


PROFILE:
Designed for families with a history of heart concerns
Balanced ratio of MUFA + PUFA with controlled saturated fats
Mild flavor → blends easily into Indian daily cooking
Moderate heat stability suitable for sautéing, shallow frying, and regular meals
BEST FOR:
 Everyday household cooking
General family meals
Neutral flavor preference
Balanced health-focused diets
Why this blend works
Combines modern heart-friendly oils with traditional Indian oils
Avoids extreme dominance of any one fat type
Supports long-term use rather than occasional specialty use
Practical for mixed cooking styles (tadka, curries, vegetables, light frying)
