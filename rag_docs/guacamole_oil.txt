TITLE: Guacamole Oil 

CATEGORY: Gut Friendly / Daily Wellness Oil Blend

COMPOSITION:
Oil	             %	
Avocado Oil        50%		
Corn Oil           40%
Walnut Oil         10%

Smoke Point: ~240–245°C
1 tbsp (14 g)~124 kcal

PROFILE:
A balanced everyday oil blend combining the stability of avocado oil with the light cooking performance of corn oil and the nutritional richness of walnut oil. Designed to provide a smooth flavor, practical cooking stability, and a more diverse fatty-acid profile that complements a balanced, fiber-rich diet.
BEST FOR:
Pairing oil with high-fiber foods
Light sautéing
Daily family meals
Stir-fries and vegetables
Mild-flavor dishes
